My semi-concerning obsession with a drink container.

By Rachel Lawrence

There's something so special about a water bottle that people actually enjoy to drink out of.

A new water bottle seems to trend every year. First it was the HydroFlask, then the Stanley Cup, but once the Owala hit the market, I was more intrigued than I usualy am by hydration fads.

I must admit, I have mild health anxiety, especially surrounding air that touches things that touch my mouth.

My old Stanley Cup filled my love of using straws, but there was no way to cover the spout. This mean that I couldn't take my bottle near the bathroom, or near a trash can, or basically anywhere with unpleasant odor in the air.

That's where the beauty of the Owala comes in. I can enjoy the joys of drinking my water with a straw without fear of contaminating myself.

This bottle is absolutey stunning, as well. I LOVE pink, and the purple and orange anccents are a great way to rope in a lovely spring aesthetic.

I purchased my bottle at target.com for about 39 dollars. It's a 40 oz FreeSip in the color KickFlip Though it was expensive, I use it every day. It's basically paid for itself!
